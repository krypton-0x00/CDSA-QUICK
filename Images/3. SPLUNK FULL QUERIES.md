## Looking for supicious process chains. (Like Notepad spawning Powershell)
```sh
index="main" sourcetype="WinEventLog:Sysmon" EventCode=1 (Image="*cmd.exe" OR Image="*powershell.exe") | stats count by ParentImage, Image
```
